about alessia carrano

I am a certified gender, relationship and sex counsellor. Combining a sociological and psychological approach I attempt to create a safe space for my clients to introspect and improve the quality of their relationships to themselves and by extension to others. I trust that I can welcome my clients in a compassionate and empathetic space in order for them to develop and strengthen their self-compassion and acceptance to eventually live freer and happier.

education

I have a Msc in Gender and Sexuality from the University of Amsterdam as well as a training in psycho-sexological counselling from France. Having lived in many places and being a mix of many different cultures I have a deep understanding of people from all types of backgrounds. I offer counseling in English, French and Italian.
